📺 How to Cast to Smart TV

🔵 For Android Users (Chromecast):

1. Install Web Video Caster or VLC Player.
2. Click the "Cast to TV" or "VLC" button above.
3. Tap the Cast Icon (📶) inside the app to connect your TV.

For iPhone/iPad (AirPlay):

1. Install VLC for Mobile.
2. Click the Orange "Play in VLC" button.
3. Tap the AirPlay icon (📺) in VLC to stream to your TV.

⚡ Pro Tip for 10GB+ Files:

For the smoothest, buffering-free experience with Dolby & DTS audio tracks, we highly recommend using VLC Player. It handles heavy uncompressed 4K files flawlessly.
