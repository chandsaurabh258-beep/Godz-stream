📺 How to Cast to Smart TV / Apple TV

🔵 For Android Users (Chromecast):

1. Install Web Video Caster or VLC Player from Play Store.
2. Click the White "Cast to TV" or Orange "VLC" button above.
3. Tap the Cast Icon (📶) inside the app and select your TV.

🍎 For iPhone/iPad Users (AirPlay):

1. Install VLC for Mobile from the App Store.
2. Click the Orange "Play in VLC" button above.
3. Click the AirPlay icon (📺) in VLC to stream to Apple TV or AirPlay 2-compatible TVs.
